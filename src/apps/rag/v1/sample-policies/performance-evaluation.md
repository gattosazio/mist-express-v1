# Performance Evaluation

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: performance_evaluation
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Evaluation Cycle

Employees are evaluated according to the company's performance review cycle and role expectations.
Supervisors may also provide interim feedback, coaching, or corrective guidance outside the formal review cycle.

## Evaluation Factors

Performance reviews consider work quality, productivity, collaboration, attendance reliability, role-specific goals, and compliance with company standards.

## Review Ownership

The employee's immediate supervisor prepares the initial performance evaluation.
Department heads review final ratings when required by department procedures.

## Improvement Plans

Employees who do not meet expectations may be placed on a performance improvement plan with defined goals, timelines, and follow-up reviews.
