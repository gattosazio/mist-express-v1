# Salary and Payroll

Department: Finance
Policy Type: salary_payroll
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Payroll Schedule

The company processes payroll twice per month.
Payroll covers approved base salary, authorized overtime, and approved recurring allowances.

## Payroll Cutoff

Timekeeping corrections, overtime approvals, and attendance adjustments must be submitted before the payroll cutoff date announced by Finance.
Items submitted after cutoff may be processed in the next payroll cycle.

## Salary Payments

Salary is paid through the employee's enrolled payroll bank account unless another approved payment method is required by law.
Employees are responsible for keeping their bank account information accurate and current.

## Payroll Concerns

Employees who identify a payroll discrepancy must report it to Finance through the payroll helpdesk.
Finance reviews payroll discrepancy reports and coordinates corrections when warranted.
