# Resignation and Clearance

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: resignation_clearance
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Resignation Notice

Employees who resign must submit a written resignation to their immediate supervisor and Human Resources.
The standard notice period is 30 calendar days unless another period is required by contract or law.

## Clearance Process

Resigning employees must complete company clearance requirements before final pay is released, subject to applicable law.
Clearance may include the return of company property, settlement of accountabilities, and completion of required exit documentation.

## Final Pay and Documents

Finance coordinates final pay processing after required clearance steps are completed and all final payroll inputs are confirmed.
Human Resources coordinates final employment documents according to company process and legal requirements.
