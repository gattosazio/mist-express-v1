# Benefits and Allowances

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: benefits_allowances
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Standard Benefits

Regular employees are eligible for government-mandated benefits and company-provided benefits according to plan rules and employment classification.
Benefit coverage and enrollment requirements are administered by Human Resources.

## Allowances

The company may provide transportation, communication, meal, or other work-related allowances depending on role and department.
An allowance is not automatically available to all employees unless this policy or a department-specific policy says so.

## Eligibility

Probationary, part-time, and project-based employees may have different benefit or allowance eligibility depending on plan terms and employment classification.
Department-specific allowances require approval and budget availability.

## Questions and Claims

Employees with questions about benefit coverage or allowance eligibility should contact Human Resources.
Claims or reimbursement-type requests must follow the process defined by the relevant policy or benefit provider.
