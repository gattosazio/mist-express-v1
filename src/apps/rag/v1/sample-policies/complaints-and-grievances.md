# Complaints and Grievances

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: complaints_grievances
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Reporting Channels

Employees may raise workplace concerns, complaints, or grievances to their immediate supervisor, Human Resources, or another authorized reporting channel when appropriate.
Complaints involving the immediate supervisor may be raised directly to Human Resources.

## Confidentiality and Retaliation

The company handles complaints as confidentially as reasonably possible.
Retaliation against an employee who raises a complaint in good faith is prohibited.

## Investigation

Human Resources reviews complaints and coordinates fact-finding with relevant stakeholders when needed.
Employees are expected to cooperate truthfully during grievance reviews and investigations.
