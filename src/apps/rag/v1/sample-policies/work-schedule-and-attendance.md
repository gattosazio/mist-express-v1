# Work Schedule and Attendance

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: work_schedule_attendance
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Standard Work Schedule

Regular full-time employees work eight hours per day, five days per week, excluding unpaid meal breaks.
The standard office schedule is Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM.
Departments may assign different shift schedules when business operations require coverage outside standard office hours.

## Timekeeping

Employees must record attendance through the company timekeeping system at the start and end of each workday.
Employees must also record overtime, undertime, and approved schedule adjustments in the same system.

## Tardiness and Absences

Employees who will be late or absent must notify their immediate supervisor as soon as possible before the start of the shift.
Repeated tardiness or unreported absences may lead to corrective action under the Disciplinary Actions policy.

## Overtime

Overtime must be approved in advance by the employee's immediate supervisor.
Overtime pay or time-off in lieu is provided only for overtime hours that were approved and properly recorded.
