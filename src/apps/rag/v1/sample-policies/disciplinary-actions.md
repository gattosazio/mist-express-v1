# Disciplinary Actions

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: disciplinary_actions
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Grounds for Discipline

Disciplinary action may result from misconduct, repeated policy violations, dishonesty, serious attendance issues, insubordination, or misuse of company property or systems.

## Corrective Measures

Depending on the severity of the issue, corrective action may include coaching, verbal warning, written warning, suspension, or termination, subject to due process and applicable law.

## Review Process

Human Resources and the employee's department management review disciplinary cases before final action is taken when required by policy or law.
The company documents disciplinary actions in the employee's record.

## Due Process

Employees are given notice of alleged violations and an opportunity to respond before final disciplinary action is imposed, unless immediate action is required for safety, security, or legal reasons.
