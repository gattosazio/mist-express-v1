# Remote Work and Flexibility

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: remote_work
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Eligibility

Remote work is allowed for regular employees whose roles can be performed effectively outside the office and whose department head supports the arrangement.
Remote work is not automatically available to probationary employees, interns, or roles that require regular on-site coverage unless an exception is approved.

## Approval

The employee's immediate supervisor reviews routine remote work requests.
The department head approves recurring or long-term remote work arrangements.

## Security Requirements

Employees working remotely must use company-approved devices when accessing internal systems that contain sensitive data.
Employees must use the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems from outside the office network.
Employees must comply with all requirements in the IT and Data Security policy while working remotely.

## Work Availability

Remote employees must remain reachable during their approved working hours and must attend required meetings unless on approved leave.
