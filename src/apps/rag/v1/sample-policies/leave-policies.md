# Leave Policies

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: leave
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Leave Requests

Employees must file leave requests through the employee self-service portal before the start of the requested leave period.
Employees should submit planned leave at least five calendar days in advance whenever practicable.

## Approval

The employee's immediate supervisor approves routine planned leave of up to three business days.
Leave longer than three business days requires approval from the department head.
Emergency leave must be reported to the immediate supervisor as soon as possible and entered in the leave system once the employee is able to do so.

## Leave Eligibility

Leave eligibility depends on employment status, accrued balances, and applicable law or company program rules.
Probationary employees may request unpaid leave subject to approval and business requirements.

## Documentation

Human Resources may require supporting documents for sick leave, bereavement leave, emergency leave, or other special leave categories when applicable.
