# Employment Status and Probation

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: employment_status_probation
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Employment Classifications

The company may engage employees as regular, probationary, project-based, fixed-term, part-time, or intern personnel.
Employment classification affects eligibility for certain benefits, leave entitlements, and flexible work arrangements.

## Probationary Period

New regular hires are subject to a six-month probationary period unless a different period is required by law or stated in the employment contract.
During probation, employees are expected to meet role-specific performance, conduct, and attendance standards.

## Regularization

An employee may be regularized upon successful completion of the probationary period and fulfillment of performance expectations.
The employee's department head and Human Resources review regularization recommendations.

## Policy Eligibility

Probationary employees may access company systems, mandatory training, and standard attendance policies.
Eligibility for remote work, certain allowances, and selected discretionary benefits may be limited during probation if another policy says so.
