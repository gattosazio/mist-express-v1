# Workplace Rules and Ethics

Department: Compliance
Policy Type: workplace_ethics
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Professional Conduct

Employees must act professionally, respectfully, and honestly in all work-related interactions.
Harassment, discrimination, retaliation, dishonesty, and misuse of company authority are prohibited.

## Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose actual or potential conflicts of interest to Compliance before participating in related business decisions.
Outside employment or business activity must not interfere with company duties or create a conflict with company interests.

## Gifts and Favors

Employees may not accept gifts, favors, or benefits that could improperly influence business decisions.
Gifts above the allowable threshold require review and approval by the Compliance Manager.

## Use of Company Resources

Company property, funds, records, and systems must be used only for legitimate business purposes and authorized personal use, if any.
