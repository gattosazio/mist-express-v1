# Training and Career Growth

Department: Human Resources
Policy Type: training_career_growth
Version: v1
Effective Date: 2026-04-26

## Mandatory Training

Employees must complete mandatory orientation, compliance, security, and role-specific training assigned by the company.
Failure to complete mandatory training by the assigned deadline may affect system access, eligibility for certain programs, or performance evaluation outcomes.

## Development Opportunities

The company may offer internal training, mentoring, certification support, and career development programs based on business needs and budget.

## Approval

Attendance at external training programs requires approval from the employee's immediate supervisor.
Department heads may require additional approval for high-cost training or travel-related training requests.

## Internal Mobility

Employees interested in internal opportunities should coordinate with their supervisor and Human Resources according to internal hiring procedures.
